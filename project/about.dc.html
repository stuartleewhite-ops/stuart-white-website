About

Who I am.

I'm Stuart. I spent twenty years in senior corporate roles and twenty years training around them, without missing a week. I coach the life I've lived.

The short version

I know the pressure, because I've worked under it.

For two decades I built and led teams inside big businesses. Long days, constant travel, real stakes. Through all of it I kept training; early mornings, hotel gyms, whatever the week allowed.

That's the difference. I'm not coaching from a textbook. I've had to make training, eating and sleeping work inside a full, demanding life, and I've done it for twenty years.

Now I help other busy people do the same, whether that's online, one to one, or in a class on a Wednesday night.

Professional experience

Twenty years leading in demanding businesses.

I've held senior roles at Johnson & Johnson, L'Oréal, Perrigo, Weetabix and Tommee Tippee. I was Global Head of Sleep at Tommee Tippee and Head of Innovation at Weetabix, and I built a Men's Health vitamin range from the ground up.

That world taught me how to turn evidence into something practical, how to build habits that survive a real schedule, and how to keep going when it would be easier to stop. It's exactly what coaching needs.
